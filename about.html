About AquaPay

We're on a Mission to Make Water Billing Simple

Founded in Chicago, AquaPay is building digital infrastructure that ensures every household can manage clean water access with dignity and ease.

Our Story

Built to Solve a Real Problem

In 2021, our founders stood in a long queue at a county water office in Chicago. They thought — there has to be a better way. AquaPay was born from that moment of frustration and a commitment to public service innovation.

Today, we serve over 500,000 households across 47 states, processing millions of dollars in payments monthly with a 99.9% uptime record.

Start Paying Now

Our Mission

To democratize access to water billing services across the United States through technology that is fast, reliable, transparent, and available to every citizen regardless of their digital literacy.

Our Vision

A United States where water utility management is entirely digital, paperless, and real-time — accessible from any device, anywhere.

Our Values

Transparency, accessibility, security, and community. We exist to serve, not just to profit.
